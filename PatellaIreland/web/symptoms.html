Symptoms

Symptoms of patellar dislocation include:

Severe pain

Pain may occur when the patella moves out of position or while trying to slide it back in. Note that pain will instantly go away once it is repositioned (see more in treatment).

Limited movement

You may be unable to move you knee fully if it is dislocated.

Swelling

The knee may swell up to protect itself from any further trauma.

Weakness

Reduced strength in the knee, in terms of kicking or lifting.
