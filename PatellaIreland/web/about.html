About Us

We are a non-profit organisation that specialise in research and analysis of patellar dislocation. Since our inception in 1998 in Wicklow, we have conducted countless studies and reports on this matter.

There is a constant thrive within us to find better treatment and diagnosis solutions for patellar dislocation. We receive no funding from the Irish Government, the EU or the HSE so any donation is greatly appreciated.





Winner of “Best knee group of the year” award 5 times in a row, from 2002-2007

Runner-up in “Best fictional organisation in an enterprise project” in 2012
