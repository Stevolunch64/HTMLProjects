Treatment

If you experience patellar dislocation, it is important to:

  • Get a medical professional to relocate it. This is done by pushing the patella back into place with both thumbs.
  • After relocation, rest the knee by keeping your leg elevated (on a cushion for example) and take some pain relief if necessary (e.g., Paracetamol, Ibuprofen).
  • Place an ice pack on the knee to help reduce swelling and stress. You may be provided with a knee brace to keep it in place.
  • Crutches can be used to move around while keeping your affected leg elevated.
  • To help the knee heal, physiotherapy may take place. This may involve stretching exercises to strengthen the leg muscles that were weakened by persistent rest.
