About Us

The F1 Limerick club was created in 2020 to have a place for F1 fans to interact with each other. At first, it started off as just a groupchat where people can discuss all things F1. However, it has evolved into hosting many public events such as quiz nights and game events.

Whether you just want to talk with like minded people, or participate in our exciting events, you are more than welcome to join. There's plenty of fun to be had here and it would be an honour if you join us on our journey.
